SHIPPING POLICY
Last updated: January 2025

=============================================================
OVERVIEW
=============================================================

We are committed to delivering your orders quickly and safely. This policy covers everything you need to know about how we ship, the carriers we use, estimated delivery windows, how to track your order, and what to do if something goes wrong.

=============================================================
1. ORDER PROCESSING TIMES
=============================================================

1.1 Standard Processing
All orders placed before 2:00 PM local warehouse time on a business day are processed the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM or on weekends and public holidays are processed on the next business day. Processing time refers to the time it takes us to pick, pack, and hand off your order to a carrier — it does not include transit time.

1.2 Extended Processing Windows
Certain items require additional processing time:
- Custom or personalized products: 3 to 5 business days
- Pre-order items: shipped on or after the stated release date
- Items marked "Ships in 5-7 business days": fulfilled from a secondary warehouse
- Large or heavy items requiring freight handling: up to 7 business days

During peak seasons (major sales events, holidays from late November through December), processing times may extend by 1 to 3 business days. We will display an estimated processing time on the product page and at checkout.

1.3 Order Confirmation
You will receive an order confirmation email immediately after purchase. Once your order is handed to a carrier, you will receive a separate shipping confirmation email with a tracking number and a link to track your package.

=============================================================
2. DOMESTIC SHIPPING OPTIONS
=============================================================

2.1 Standard Shipping
Estimated delivery: 5 to 7 business days after processing.
Available for all addresses within the continental United States, Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories (Puerto Rico, Guam, U.S. Virgin Islands). Free on orders over $49. A flat fee of $5.99 applies to orders below $49.

2.2 Expedited Shipping
Estimated delivery: 2 to 3 business days after processing.
Available for most items in the continental United States. Not available for freight items, hazardous materials, or oversized products. Fee: $12.99 flat or calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

2.3 Overnight Shipping
Estimated delivery: 1 business day after processing (orders must be placed before 11:00 AM).
Available Monday through Thursday (Friday orders may not arrive until Monday). Fee: $24.99 or calculated at checkout. Not available for all product categories or remote zip codes.

2.4 Same-Day Delivery
Available in select metropolitan areas through a third-party courier network. Orders must be placed before 10:00 AM. A delivery fee of $14.99 applies. Available items are marked with a "Same-Day Eligible" badge on the product page.

2.5 Store Pickup (BOPIS)
Buy Online, Pick Up In Store is available at participating locations. Select your store at checkout. You will receive a "Ready for Pickup" notification, typically within 2 hours on business days. Orders not collected within 7 days are cancelled and refunded.

=============================================================
3. INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING
=============================================================

3.1 Countries We Ship To
We ship to over 80 countries worldwide. The full list is available at checkout when you enter your delivery address. If your country is not listed, we currently do not offer shipping there.

3.2 International Delivery Windows
- Canada and Mexico: 7 to 14 business days
- Europe (EU and UK): 10 to 18 business days
- Asia-Pacific: 14 to 21 business days
- Latin America: 14 to 25 business days
- Rest of World: 18 to 30 business days

These are estimates. Actual delivery times depend on customs clearance, local postal infrastructure, and carrier capacity.

3.3 International Shipping Fees
Fees are calculated at checkout based on destination, package weight, and declared value. A shipping quote is displayed before you confirm payment. Fees are non-refundable once the shipment is dispatched.

3.4 Customs, Duties, and Import Taxes
Import duties, taxes, and brokerage fees charged by the destination country are the sole responsibility of the recipient. We are not able to predict or control these charges. We recommend checking with your country's customs authority before ordering. We declare the full commercial value on all customs forms and cannot mark items as gifts or undervalue them.

3.5 Restricted Items for International Shipping
Certain product categories cannot be shipped internationally due to customs regulations, hazardous material classifications, or carrier restrictions. These include but are not limited to: lithium batteries above certain watt-hours, aerosol products, certain food and supplement items, and products restricted by trade sanctions. Restrictions are noted on the product page.

=============================================================
4. SHIPMENT TRACKING
=============================================================

4.1 How to Track Your Order
Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number by email. You can track your order by:
- Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
- Logging into your account and navigating to Order History
- Entering your tracking number on our website's Order Tracking page
- Using the carrier's website directly

4.2 Tracking Updates
Tracking information may take up to 24 hours to appear after the carrier scans the package. Tracking events are provided directly by the carrier and may not update in real time during transit.

4.3 Tracking Not Updating
If your tracking information has not updated in more than 3 business days (domestic) or 7 business days (international), contact our support team. We will open an investigation with the carrier.

=============================================================
5. SHIPPING TO P.O. BOXES AND MILITARY ADDRESSES
=============================================================

We can ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS for most standard items. FedEx and UPS deliveries cannot be made to P.O. Boxes. We ship to APO, FPO, and DPO military addresses via USPS. Delivery to military addresses may take 3 to 6 weeks depending on the location. Expedited options are not available for military addresses.

=============================================================
6. SPLIT SHIPMENTS AND PARTIAL ORDERS
=============================================================

If your order contains items from multiple warehouses or includes a pre-order item alongside an in-stock item, it may ship in multiple packages at no additional cost to you. Each shipment will have its own tracking number. You will be notified by email for each individual shipment.

=============================================================
7. DAMAGED, LOST, OR STOLEN PACKAGES
=============================================================

7.1 Damaged in Transit
If your package arrives visibly damaged, please take photos of the outer packaging and the item before unpacking further. Contact our support team within 7 days of delivery with your order number and photos. We will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to you.

7.2 Lost Packages (Domestic)
If your tracking shows "delivered" but you have not received your package:
- Wait 24 hours — packages are sometimes scanned before delivery
- Check with neighbors and building management
- Contact the carrier using your tracking number
If the package is still not found after these steps, contact us within 15 days of the expected delivery date. We will file a claim with the carrier and issue a replacement or refund pending investigation results.

7.3 Lost Packages (International)
For international shipments, please allow the full estimated delivery window plus 7 additional business days before reporting a lost package. International investigations can take 4 to 6 weeks to resolve.

7.4 Stolen Packages (Porch Piracy)
We are not liable for packages confirmed as delivered to the correct address that are subsequently stolen. We strongly recommend requiring a signature at delivery (available at checkout) or shipping to a secure location. As a courtesy, we may offer a one-time replacement at our discretion.

=============================================================
8. SHIPPING RESTRICTIONS
=============================================================

Some items cannot be shipped to certain states or regions due to local regulations. For example, certain electronics, batteries, and chemical-based products have restrictions. These are clearly noted on the product page. If you place an order containing a restricted item for a restricted destination, we will notify you and offer a refund or substitution.

=============================================================
9. SHIPPING FEE REFUNDS
=============================================================

Shipping fees are non-refundable once a shipment is dispatched, except in cases where:
- The carrier loses the package during transit (confirmed by carrier investigation)
- The item arrives damaged due to transit mishandling
- We shipped the wrong item or the item was defective prior to shipment

=============================================================
10. CONTACT FOR SHIPPING ISSUES
=============================================================

For any shipping-related questions or issues, contact our support team through live chat, email at support@shopexample.com, or by phone Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM. Please have your order number ready.
