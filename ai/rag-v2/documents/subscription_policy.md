# Subscription Policy
_Last updated: January 2025_


## Overview

This policy covers all recurring subscription plans offered on our platform, including how billing works, how to manage your subscription, cancellation rights, pausing options, plan changes, and refund eligibility specific to subscriptions.


## 1. Available Subscription Plans

### 1.1 Basic Plan
Monthly fee: $9.99/month or $99.99/year (save 17%)
Includes: Free standard shipping on every order, early access to select sales, and 5% cashback on purchases applied as store credit.

### 1.2 Premium Plan
Monthly fee: $19.99/month or $199.99/year (save 17%)
Includes everything in Basic, plus: Free expedited shipping on orders over $35, priority customer support, access to members-only flash deals, extended 60-day returns, and 10% cashback on purchases.

### 1.3 Family Plan
Monthly fee: $29.99/month or $299.99/year (save 17%)
Includes everything in Premium, plus: Up to 5 linked accounts, shared membership benefits across all linked accounts, and 12% cashback.

### 1.4 Business Plan
Custom pricing based on usage volume and team size. Includes all Premium benefits, a dedicated account manager, consolidated invoicing, and tax-exempt purchasing options. Contact enterprise@shopexample.com for a quote.


## 2. Billing And Payment

### 2.1 Billing Cycle
Monthly plans renew automatically every 30 days from the date of initial purchase. Annual plans renew automatically 12 months from the date of initial purchase. Your billing date is the same calendar date each month (or year) as when you first subscribed. For example, if you subscribed on January 15, you will be billed on February 15, March 15, and so on.

### 2.2 Payment Methods Accepted
- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- PayPal
- Shop Pay
- ACH bank transfer (Business Plan only)

We do not currently accept prepaid gift cards as a primary payment method for subscriptions.

### 2.3 Automatic Renewal
Subscriptions renew automatically to ensure uninterrupted service. We will send a renewal reminder email 7 days before your annual renewal date. Monthly renewals do not generate a pre-renewal email, but you will receive a receipt after each renewal charge.

### 2.4 Failed Payment
If a subscription payment fails, we will:
- Retry the charge after 3 days
- Retry again after 7 days
- Notify you by email after each failed attempt with instructions to update your payment method
- Suspend your subscription if payment fails after 10 days from the original due date

During a payment failure grace period (up to 10 days), your subscription benefits remain active. If your subscription is suspended due to non-payment, you can reactivate it at any time by updating your payment method — your billing cycle restarts from the date of reactivation.

### 2.5 Price Changes
We reserve the right to change subscription pricing. We will notify you at least 30 days in advance of any price increase via email. The new price takes effect at your next renewal date following the notice period. If you do not agree with the new pricing, you may cancel before the renewal date at no charge.


## 3. Managing Your Subscription

### 3.1 Accessing Subscription Settings
Log into your account and go to Account > Subscriptions to:
- View your current plan and billing date
- Update your payment method
- Upgrade or downgrade your plan
- Pause your subscription
- Cancel your subscription

### 3.2 Updating Payment Information
You can update your payment method at any time in Account > Subscriptions > Payment Method. Changes take effect immediately and are used for the next renewal charge. Your existing billing cycle is not affected.

### 3.3 Viewing Billing History
Your full billing history, including all subscription charges and receipts, is available in Account > Billing History. You can download individual receipts as PDFs for expense reporting.


## 4. Plan Changes (Upgrades And Downgrades)

### 4.1 Upgrading Your Plan
You can upgrade from Basic to Premium or Family at any time. The price difference for the remaining days in your current billing cycle is calculated on a prorated basis and charged immediately. Your new plan benefits activate immediately.

Example: If you are on Basic ($9.99/month) and upgrade to Premium ($19.99/month) with 15 days remaining in your cycle, you will be charged approximately $5.00 immediately (half the $10 monthly difference). Your next full renewal will be at the Premium rate.

### 4.2 Downgrading Your Plan
You can downgrade your plan at any time. The downgrade takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you continue to enjoy your current plan's benefits until then. No refund is issued for the remaining days of the higher-tier plan.

### 4.3 Switching Between Monthly and Annual Billing
Switching from monthly to annual billing can be done at any time. You will be charged the annual rate immediately, and your billing cycle resets to the date of the switch. If you switch from annual to monthly, the change takes effect at the end of your current annual period.


## 5. Pausing Your Subscription

### 5.1 Pause Eligibility
Subscription pausing is available for accounts that have been active for at least 60 days. You may pause your subscription for a minimum of 1 month and a maximum of 3 consecutive months.

### 5.2 How to Pause
Go to Account > Subscriptions > Pause Subscription. Select the number of months to pause (1, 2, or 3). Your subscription and billing are suspended immediately. Benefits are deactivated during the pause period. At the end of the pause, your subscription resumes and billing restarts automatically on the same schedule.

### 5.3 Pause Limits
You may pause your subscription a maximum of once per 12-month period. Pausing a subscription does not affect your account history, order history, or accumulated store credit.

### 5.4 Early Resume
You may resume a paused subscription early at any time in Account > Subscriptions > Resume. Your billing cycle restarts from the date of early resume.


## 6. Cancellation

### 6.1 How to Cancel
You can cancel at any time through Account > Subscriptions > Cancel. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — there is no charge for cancelling, and you retain full benefits until your period ends.

### 6.2 Cancellation for Annual Plans
If you cancel an annual plan, your benefits remain active through the end of the paid annual period. No prorated refund is provided for unused months of an annual plan, except during the initial refund window (see Section 7).

### 6.3 Cancellation Confirmation
You will receive a cancellation confirmation email immediately. If you do not receive this email within a few minutes, check your spam folder. If you still do not receive it, contact support — do not assume the cancellation was processed.

### 6.4 Re-subscribing After Cancellation
You can re-subscribe at any time after cancellation by visiting Account > Subscriptions. If you re-subscribe within 30 days of cancellation, your original billing date and any loyalty progress may be restored.


## 7. Refund Policy For Subscriptions

### 7.1 New Subscription Refund Window
For new subscriptions (first-time subscribers), a full refund is available within 7 days of the initial charge if you have not used any subscription benefits (e.g., free shipping, cashback redemption, or priority support access). Contact support@shopexample.com to request this refund.

### 7.2 Renewal Refunds
Refunds for subscription renewals are available within 7 days of the renewal charge if you have not used any benefits since the renewal date. This is available once per 12-month period per account.

### 7.3 No Prorated Refunds
We do not provide prorated refunds for partially used subscription periods outside of the 7-day windows described above. If you cancel on day 15 of a 30-day cycle, your benefits remain active through day 30, but no refund is issued for the unused 15 days.

### 7.4 Exceptions
Refunds outside the standard windows may be considered in exceptional circumstances, such as:
- Documented medical emergency preventing use
- Technical failure on our side preventing access to benefits
- Duplicate charges caused by our billing system error

Submit exception requests to billing@shopexample.com with supporting documentation.


## 8. Shared And Family Plan Accounts

### 8.1 Linking Accounts
Family Plan subscribers can link up to 5 accounts. The primary account holder controls the plan and billing. Linked accounts share all membership benefits but have separate order histories, addresses, and payment methods.

### 8.2 Removing Linked Accounts
The primary account holder can remove a linked account at any time from Account > Subscriptions > Manage Family. Removed accounts lose subscription benefits immediately.

### 8.3 Transfer of Ownership
The primary account role can be transferred to another linked account member. Both parties must confirm the transfer via email. Transfers take effect within 24 hours.


## 9. Subscription Benefits Details

### 9.1 Cashback Store Credit
Cashback is credited to your account within 5 business days after your order is delivered and the return window closes. Cashback is applied as store credit. Store credit does not expire and can be used on any future order. Cashback is not earned on store credit purchases, gift cards, shipping fees, or taxes.

### 9.2 Free Shipping Eligibility
Free shipping is applied automatically at checkout when logged in with an active subscription. Free shipping applies to the carrier method selected at checkout (standard or expedited, depending on plan). Overnight and same-day shipping are not included in free shipping benefits.

### 9.3 Extended Returns
Premium and Family plan members receive a 60-day return window on all eligible purchases made while the subscription is active, versus the standard 30-day window.

### 9.4 Flash Deals Access
Members-only flash deals are sent via email and in-app notifications. Access is only available during the active deal window (typically 24 to 72 hours). Items sold in flash deals are limited quantity.


## 10. Contact For Subscription Issues

For billing questions, plan changes, or subscription issues, contact our billing team at billing@shopexample.com or call 1-800-555-0199. Subscription settings are also manageable 24/7 in Account > Subscriptions without needing to contact support.
