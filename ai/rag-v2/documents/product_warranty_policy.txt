PRODUCT WARRANTY POLICY
Last updated: January 2025

=============================================================
OVERVIEW
=============================================================

This policy explains the warranty coverage we offer on products sold on our platform, how to file a warranty claim, what is and is not covered, and how warranty service is performed. Note that many products also carry separate manufacturer warranties — this policy covers our store warranty, which may run alongside or extend the manufacturer's warranty.

=============================================================
1. STORE WARRANTY COVERAGE
=============================================================

1.1 What Is the Store Warranty?
Our store warranty is a promise that the products we sell are free from manufacturing defects and will perform as described for the duration of the coverage period. It applies to physical products sold directly by us (not third-party sellers on our marketplace, unless specifically noted).

1.2 Standard Coverage Period by Category
- Consumer electronics (phones, tablets, laptops, cameras): 1 year from delivery date
- Home appliances (large and small): 1 year from delivery date
- Audio equipment (speakers, headphones): 1 year from delivery date
- Furniture and home decor: 6 months from delivery date
- Clothing and footwear: 90 days from delivery date (manufacturing defects only — not wear and tear)
- Tools and hardware: 1 year from delivery date
- Toys and games: 90 days from delivery date
- Sports and outdoor equipment: 6 months from delivery date

Extended warranty periods may apply to items specifically marked "Extended Store Warranty" on the product page.

1.3 Coverage Details
Our store warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects in materials or workmanship
- Failure to perform as described in the product listing under normal use
- Components that fail prematurely under normal operating conditions

=============================================================
2. WHAT IS NOT COVERED
=============================================================

Our store warranty does not cover:

2.1 Physical Damage
- Cracks, dents, or breaks caused by dropping, impact, or mishandling
- Damage caused by liquid exposure (unless the product is specifically rated waterproof)
- Scratches or cosmetic damage resulting from normal use

2.2 Misuse and Unauthorized Modifications
- Damage caused by use inconsistent with the product's intended purpose
- Modifications, repairs, or alterations made by anyone other than an authorized service center
- Use of non-approved accessories, attachments, or power supplies that caused damage

2.3 Environmental and Accidental Damage
- Damage from power surges, floods, fire, or other environmental events
- Damage resulting from failure to follow product care instructions
- Consumable parts that wear out through normal use (batteries, filters, blades, bulbs)

2.4 Cosmetic Wear
- Natural color changes or fading from sunlight or washing
- Scuffs, surface scratches, or worn finish from daily use
- Fraying on clothing or footwear from regular wear

2.5 Theft or Loss
Our warranty does not cover lost or stolen items. We offer optional product protection plans (see Section 7) that may cover theft in some categories.

=============================================================
3. MANUFACTURER WARRANTY
=============================================================

3.1 Relationship to Store Warranty
Many products include a manufacturer warranty that runs separately from our store warranty. In some cases the manufacturer warranty is longer (2 or 3 years). You are entitled to both our store warranty and the manufacturer warranty — whichever is more favorable to you for the specific issue.

3.2 Finding Manufacturer Warranty Information
Manufacturer warranty terms are usually:
- Included in the product packaging documentation
- Listed on the manufacturer's website
- Summarized on the product page under "Warranty & Support"

3.3 Contacting the Manufacturer
For defects that fall under the manufacturer warranty, you can contact the manufacturer directly using the information in your product documentation. We can also assist you in initiating a manufacturer warranty claim by providing purchase verification documentation.

=============================================================
4. HOW TO FILE A WARRANTY CLAIM
=============================================================

4.1 Before You File
Gather the following:
- Your order number
- Photos or a short video demonstrating the defect or issue
- A description of when and how the issue first appeared
- The product's model number or serial number (usually on the product or packaging)

4.2 Filing a Claim Online
1. Log into your account and go to Order History
2. Select the order containing the product
3. Click "File Warranty Claim"
4. Describe the issue, upload photos/video, and submit
5. You will receive a case number and a response within 2 business days

4.3 Filing a Claim by Email
Email warranty@shopexample.com with your order number, product details, description of the defect, and any photos or video. A warranty specialist will respond within 2 business days.

4.4 What Happens After Filing
Our warranty team reviews your claim and may:
- Approve the claim immediately based on your description and evidence
- Request a video call to assess the issue in real time
- Ask you to ship the product to an authorized service center for inspection

=============================================================
5. WARRANTY RESOLUTION OPTIONS
=============================================================

Depending on the nature and severity of the defect, we will offer one of the following resolutions:

5.1 Replacement
A new or certified refurbished unit of the same product (or a direct equivalent if the exact model is no longer available) is shipped at no cost to you. Return of the defective item is required before or after the replacement ships, depending on item value. We provide a prepaid return label.

5.2 Repair
For certain categories (large appliances, electronics), we may arrange repair by an authorized service center. We coordinate pickup/drop-off and cover all costs for warranty-covered repairs. Repair typically takes 7 to 14 business days.

5.3 Partial Refund or Store Credit
If neither replacement nor repair is suitable (e.g., product discontinued, minor defect), we may offer a partial refund or store credit based on the product's original price and the severity of the defect.

5.4 Full Refund
In cases of significant defects that cannot be resolved through replacement or repair, we will offer a full refund to your original payment method.

=============================================================
6. SHIPPING FOR WARRANTY SERVICE
=============================================================

6.1 Return Shipping
We provide a prepaid return shipping label for all warranty claims approved under our store warranty. Do not ship the item before receiving the label.

6.2 Packaging Requirements
Pack the item securely. Use the original packaging if available. Include the defective unit only — do not include accessories unless specifically requested by the warranty team. If you discard the original packaging, use a sturdy box with appropriate padding.

6.3 International Warranty Claims
For orders shipped internationally, warranty service may be handled in coordination with a local service partner. International return shipping costs may be reimbursed after the claim is resolved. Contact warranty@shopexample.com for specific guidance based on your location.

=============================================================
7. EXTENDED PROTECTION PLANS
=============================================================

7.1 What Is an Extended Protection Plan?
An optional protection plan extends coverage beyond the standard store warranty and typically adds accidental damage protection (drops, spills) not covered by the standard warranty. Plans are offered at checkout for eligible product categories.

7.2 Coverage Options
- 1-Year Extended Protection: adds 1 year to the standard warranty period
- 2-Year Extended Protection: adds 2 years to the standard warranty period
- Accidental Damage Protection: covers drops, spills, and accidental breakage (not available for all categories)

7.3 Plan Terms
Protection plans are administered by a third-party insurer (listed in your plan documentation). Claims under the protection plan are filed through the same process as standard warranty claims. Our team assists with the process.

7.4 Plan Cost
Prices vary by product price and category. The plan cost is displayed at checkout. Protection plans are non-refundable after 30 days.

=============================================================
8. THIRD-PARTY SELLER PRODUCTS
=============================================================

Products sold by third-party sellers through our marketplace are covered only by the manufacturer's warranty unless the seller explicitly states otherwise. We do not extend our store warranty to third-party seller listings. For third-party seller warranty issues, contact the seller directly through your Order History > Contact Seller. If you are unable to resolve the issue with the seller, contact our marketplace support team for assistance.

=============================================================
9. PROOF OF PURCHASE
=============================================================

Your order confirmation email or receipt serves as proof of purchase for warranty purposes. We recommend keeping a copy. If you purchased as a guest and no longer have your receipt, contact support@shopexample.com with the email address used for purchase and the approximate order date — we may be able to locate the order.

=============================================================
10. WARRANTY FRAUD
=============================================================

Submitting fraudulent warranty claims (e.g., claiming a product is defective when it has been physically damaged by the customer, or filing claims for products not purchased from us) may result in account suspension, denial of future warranty claims, and, in serious cases, legal action.
