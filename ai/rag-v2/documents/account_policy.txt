ACCOUNT MANAGEMENT POLICY
Last updated: January 2025

=============================================================
OVERVIEW
=============================================================

This document covers everything related to your customer account: how to create one, how to manage your personal information, security best practices, what happens if you lose access, and your rights regarding account data and deletion.

=============================================================
1. CREATING AN ACCOUNT
=============================================================

1.1 Who Can Create an Account
Accounts can be created by individuals aged 18 or older (or the minimum age of majority in your jurisdiction). By creating an account, you confirm that you meet the age requirement and agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

1.2 Registration Options
You can create an account by:
- Registering directly with an email address and password on our website or app
- Signing in with Google (uses your Google account credentials)
- Signing in with Apple (uses your Apple ID)
- Signing in with Facebook

When using a social login, we receive only the basic profile information permitted by that platform (typically your name and email). We do not receive your social media password.

1.3 Account Verification
After registration with email and password, a verification email is sent to the address you provided. You must verify your email before you can place orders. The verification link is valid for 48 hours. If it expires, request a new one from the sign-in page.

1.4 One Account Per Person
Each person may maintain one active account. Creating multiple accounts to circumvent restrictions (such as one-time promotions or return policy limits) is a violation of our Terms of Service and may result in all associated accounts being suspended.

=============================================================
2. MANAGING YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION
=============================================================

2.1 Name and Contact Information
Your name and email address can be updated in Account > Personal Information. Email changes require verification of the new address before taking effect.

2.2 Delivery Addresses
You can save up to 10 delivery addresses in your address book. Set a default address that is pre-selected at checkout. Addresses are stored securely and never shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

2.3 Communication Preferences
Manage your email preferences in Account > Notifications. Options include:
- Order and shipping notifications (required, cannot be disabled)
- Promotional emails and sale announcements
- Subscription reminders and billing alerts
- New product announcements
- Community forum digests

You can also unsubscribe from marketing emails via the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any marketing email. This does not affect transactional emails (receipts, shipping updates).

2.4 Profile Photo
An optional profile photo can be uploaded in Account > Personal Information. Photos must be appropriate for a public-facing platform and not include trademarks or copyrighted material.

=============================================================
3. ACCOUNT SECURITY
=============================================================

3.1 Password Requirements
Passwords must be at least 8 characters and include at least one uppercase letter, one lowercase letter, one number, and one special character. We recommend using a unique password not used on other services.

3.2 Changing Your Password
Go to Account > Security > Change Password. You must enter your current password to set a new one. If you do not know your current password, use the "Forgot Password" flow. After a password change, all active sessions on other devices are logged out.

3.3 Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)
We strongly recommend enabling 2FA in Account > Security > Two-Factor Authentication. Options:
- SMS code to your phone number
- Authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or any TOTP-compatible app)
- Email code as a fallback

When 2FA is enabled, you will be prompted for a verification code at each login. High-value orders may require 2FA verification regardless of whether it is enabled on your account.

3.4 Login Activity
Review recent login activity in Account > Security > Login History. Each entry shows the date, time, device type, and approximate location. If you see a login you do not recognize, change your password immediately and contact security@shopexample.com.

3.5 Session Management
You can view and terminate active sessions on other devices in Account > Security > Active Sessions. This is useful if you left yourself logged in on a shared computer.

3.6 Suspicious Activity
If you receive an email or message claiming to be from us asking for your password, payment info, or personal data, do not respond. We will never ask for your password. Report phishing attempts to security@shopexample.com.

=============================================================
4. FORGOT PASSWORD AND ACCOUNT RECOVERY
=============================================================

4.1 Forgot Password Flow
On the sign-in page, click "Forgot Password" and enter the email address associated with your account. A password reset link will be sent to that email. The link is valid for 1 hour. If you do not receive the email, check spam and ensure you entered the correct address.

4.2 Account Locked
After 10 consecutive failed login attempts, your account is temporarily locked for 30 minutes as a security measure. After the lockout period, you may try again or use the forgot password flow.

4.3 Lost Access to Email
If you no longer have access to the email address on your account:
1. Contact support@shopexample.com from any email address
2. Provide: your account email, a recent order number, and the delivery address used on that order
3. We will verify your identity using order history and ask security questions
4. Upon successful verification, we can update your email address

For accounts with 2FA enabled, you will also need to provide a backup code (generated when 2FA was set up) if your phone is unavailable.

4.4 Social Login Recovery
If you signed up with Google or Apple and have lost access to that account, contact the respective provider to recover your social account, then use it to log in. If you are unable to recover the social account, contact our support team for manual identity verification.

=============================================================
5. ORDER HISTORY AND WISHLIST
=============================================================

5.1 Order History
All orders placed while logged into your account are stored in Order History indefinitely. You can view order details, track shipments, initiate returns, and download receipts. Guest orders are not linked to an account.

5.2 Wishlist
Save items to your Wishlist for future purchase. Wishlists can be made public (shareable via link) or private. Public wishlists display only product names and prices — never your personal details. You can create up to 5 named wishlists.

5.3 Recently Viewed
Your recently viewed items are stored in your account to personalize your browsing experience. This data is retained for 90 days. You can clear your browsing history in Account > Privacy > Clear Browsing Data.

=============================================================
6. LOYALTY AND REWARDS
=============================================================

6.1 Points Earning
Registered account holders earn 1 loyalty point per $1 spent (excluding taxes, shipping, and gift card purchases). Points are credited to your account after the return window for the relevant order closes.

6.2 Points Redemption
Points can be redeemed at checkout (100 points = $1 in discount). Minimum redemption: 500 points. Points cannot be combined with certain promotional discounts (check terms at checkout).

6.3 Points Expiry
Points expire 24 months after they are earned if no purchase activity occurs on the account. Making any purchase resets the expiry clock for all accumulated points.

6.4 Tier Status
Tiers are determined by total spend in a rolling 12-month period:
- Silver: $0 to $499 — base earning rate
- Gold: $500 to $1,499 — 1.25x earning rate, extended returns
- Platinum: $1,500 and above — 1.5x earning rate, priority support, free expedited shipping

Tier status is evaluated monthly. Dropping below a tier threshold results in reclassification at the next monthly evaluation.

=============================================================
7. ACCOUNT SUSPENSION AND TERMINATION
=============================================================

7.1 Grounds for Suspension
We may suspend or terminate accounts that:
- Violate our Terms of Service or any policy
- Are associated with fraudulent activity or chargebacks in bad faith
- Are used to create fake reviews or manipulate ratings
- Abuse promotions, discounts, or the return policy
- Attempt to access restricted areas of our systems

7.2 Notification
We will make reasonable efforts to notify you by email before suspending an account, unless the activity poses an immediate security risk or is clearly fraudulent.

7.3 Appealing a Suspension
If you believe your account was suspended in error, email appeals@shopexample.com with your account email and a description of the situation. Appeals are reviewed within 5 business days.

=============================================================
8. ACCOUNT DELETION AND DATA RIGHTS
=============================================================

8.1 How to Delete Your Account
To request account deletion, go to Account > Privacy > Delete Account, or email privacy@shopexample.com. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.

8.2 What Happens to Your Data
Upon deletion:
- Personal profile information is removed from our systems
- Order history is anonymized but retained for legal and financial record-keeping (typically 7 years)
- Stored payment methods are immediately removed
- Wishlist, loyalty points, and subscription are terminated
- Any outstanding refunds owed to you will still be processed

8.3 Data Export
Before deleting, you can request a copy of your personal data in Account > Privacy > Download My Data. We will prepare a downloadable file within 14 days.

8.4 Right to Correction
You have the right to correct inaccurate personal information stored on your account at any time through Account > Personal Information, or by contacting privacy@shopexample.com.
